Introductory Questions:

How are you feeling today?
Is there anything specific you'd like to talk about?
What are your thoughts and emotions right now?
Prompts for Deepening the Conversation:

Can you tell me more about that?
What do you think is causing you to feel this way?
Have you experienced something similar before?
How does this make you feel physically?
What are your coping mechanisms for dealing with these feelings?
Encouraging Self-Reflection:

What do you think you need right now?
What are your goals for this conversation?
How can I support you best today?
Affirmations and Validation:

It's okay to feel this way.
Your feelings are valid.
You're not alone in this.
